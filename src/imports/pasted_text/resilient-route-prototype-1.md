You are an expert full‑stack engineer and hackathon winner. Build a complete, production‑ready prototype called **“ResilientRoute”** – a supply chain disruption preemption and dynamic route optimizer. The project must be completed entirely inside a Google Cloud environment and be demo‑ready in 48 hours.

## 1. Core Problem to Solve
- Global supply chains lose billions because planners cannot predict how weather & bottlenecks cascade across multi‑modal lanes until after delays happen.
- Planners need a real‑time, predictive dashboard that shows disruption risk, instantly recommends alternative routes with cost/time/carbon tradeoffs, and allows one‑click approval.

## 2. Must‑Have Features
- **Real‑time disruption detection**: Fuse public weather/climate data with synthetic shipment telemetry to predict lane delay probability 72h ahead.
- **Dynamic route replanning**: For any at‑risk shipment, generate 3 alternative multi‑modal routes (fastest, cheapest, lowest carbon) using real routable paths.
- **AI‑explained recommendations**: Every suggested reroute comes with a natural‑language rationale generated by Gemini (Vertex AI).
- **Interactive dashboard (IMPRESS THE JUDGES)**:
  - A dark‑theme, futuristic map (Google Maps JavaScript API) with live shipment icons and glowing lane risk colours (green→yellow→red).
  - Click a shipment to see current vs. proposed route overlayed, a timeline slider showing ETA probability shifts, and a bar chart comparing cost / time / CO₂.
  - A big "Approve Reroute" button that instantly updates the shipment state.
  - A "What‑If" sandbox: drag a storm or port closure onto the map and watch the risk propagate in real time.
- **Closed‑loop execution**: After approval, show a simulated carrier confirmation and update the map.
